NamazShield

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Overview

NamazShield is built with privacy as a core principle. Your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to any server.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • Your name, age, and onboarding answers are stored only on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Your prayer times are calculated locally on your device using your location coordinates. Your location is never sent to any server.
  • Your selected apps for shielding are stored on your device using Apple's FamilyControls framework. We cannot see which apps you select.
  • Your prayer streaks, journal entries, and garden progress are stored only on your device.

Screen Time and FamilyControls

NamazShield uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls and DeviceActivity) to shield selected apps during prayer times. This data is managed entirely by Apple's frameworks on your device. NamazShield cannot read your app usage data, browsing history, or any other Screen Time information. We only use the API to apply and remove shields on apps you explicitly choose.

Location

NamazShield requests your location to calculate accurate prayer times for your area. Your coordinates are cached locally on your device and are never transmitted to any external server. Prayer time calculations are performed entirely on-device using the open-source Adhan library.

Subscriptions

NamazShield offers a subscription managed through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. Subscription status is verified through RevenueCat, a third-party service that communicates with Apple's servers. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app user ID but no personal information. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.

Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services. No data is sent to Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, or any similar service.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat - Subscription management. Receives anonymous user ID only. Privacy policy
  • Apple Screen Time API - App shielding. All data stays on device under Apple's control.

Data Retention

All data is stored on your device. If you delete the app, all data is deleted. We have no server-side data to retain or delete.

Children's Privacy

NamazShield is not directed at children under 13. It is an adult self-regulation tool. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page with a revised date. We will not retroactively change how we handle data you have already provided.
