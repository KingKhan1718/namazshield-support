Description of Service

NamazShield is an iOS application that uses Apple's Screen Time API to temporarily shield selected apps during Islamic prayer times. The app calculates prayer times based on your location and allows you to confirm prayers to remove shields. NamazShield also provides supplementary content including Quran verses, duas, a prayer journal, and a personal garden that grows with your consistency.

Prayer Times and Content

Prayer times are calculated using the open-source Adhan library based on your geographic coordinates and selected calculation method. While we strive for accuracy, NamazShield is a digital tool and should not be your sole source for determining prayer times. Screen Time and App Blocking

NamazShield uses Apple's FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity frameworks to shield apps. The effectiveness of app shielding depends on Apple's Screen Time API and your device settings. NamazShield is not responsible for any disruptions caused by app shielding, including missed notifications or inability to access apps during emergencies. You can always disable shielding through the app's settings.
