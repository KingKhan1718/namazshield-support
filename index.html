Frequently Asked Questions

How does NamazShield block my apps?

NamazShield uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls) to temporarily shield the apps you choose during prayer times. Everything runs on your device. We never see your data.

Can I choose which prayers activate the shield?

Yes. Go to Settings and tap Shield Settings. You can toggle each of the five prayers on or off individually.

What is the grace period?

The grace period gives you a few minutes after adhan before your apps lock, so you can finish what you are doing. You can set it to 0, 5, 10, or 15 minutes in Shield Settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, tap Subscriptions, find NamazShield, and tap Cancel Subscription.
